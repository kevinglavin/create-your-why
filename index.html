Create Your Why helps you navigate complex career and life transitions using narrative psychology and storytelling techniques to design and redesign yourself in today's ever-changing world of work.

What We Do

We help our clients address complex career/life transitions and challenges that cannot be addressed using traditional career theories and techniques. Using post-modern career interventions, we focus on the power of story to help clients design and re-design themselves in the ever-changing world of work that characterizes the 21st century.

We teach you how to construct, narrate, and voice your own story. Sometimes, we need to show you how to deconstruct a story that no longer works for you. The end result is a new story/chapter you can use to address and navigate your current career concern, challenge, or transition.

Finding Yourself in Transition (FYiT)

The first and only online version of the Career Construction Interview (CCI), a powerful tool for self-discovery, life planning, and career guidance. Developed by world-renowned vocational psychologist Professor Mark Savickas, the CCI is backed by extensive research and addresses both life and career transitions.
