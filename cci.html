Finding Yourself in Transition

A spoken Career Construction Interview, guided in Dr. Kevin Glavin's style.

This is a voice conversation — about 45 minutes when you have the time to do it properly. There are no wrong answers. You'll be asked about people you admired, what you love to watch, a favourite story, a saying, and a few early memories. Find a quiet spot, and we'll take it slowly.

  • Your microphone is used only during the conversation.
  • Your answers are saved so you can revisit them and get your report.
  • You can stop any time.
